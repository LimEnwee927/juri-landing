The problem

Legal advice in Malaysia is slow, expensive, and out of reach for most individuals and SMEs. Generic AI tools make it worse: they're trained on foreign law, send sensitive data to third-party servers, and ignore that Malaysia runs two parallel legal systems — Civil law and Syariah law — that most off-the-shelf AI has never been built to understand.

Why Juri

Privacy by design

Juri is built to protect data privacy at every step — client documents and queries are handled with strict safeguards designed for sensitive legal data.

Built for Malaysia's dual legal system

Civil law coverage — starting with the Employment Act and Contract Law — is live today. Syariah/Muslim law coverage is in active development. See the roadmap.

Affordable by design

Built on cost-efficient, high-speed open models — Juri is priced to be a fraction of the cost of a traditional legal consultation.
